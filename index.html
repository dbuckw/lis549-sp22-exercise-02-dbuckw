Description

Philadelphia is the largest city in Pennsylvania, and the sixth-largest city in the United States. Nicknamed the City of Brotherly Love, Philadelphia was the first capital of the United States and is known for hosting the Second Continental Congress--which signed the Declaration of Independence--as well as the Constitution Convention.
